Pinfold is a productivity and business file-management app for Android. Browse and secure files on your device, back up to your own Google Drive / OneDrive / Dropbox accounts, and publish to social platforms.

Built for everyday users and creators
  • File explorer with search, filters, and vault encryption
  • App lock with PIN and biometric unlock
  • Backup to your Google Drive, OneDrive, or Dropbox (your accounts — not Pinfold storage)
  • Social Publishing Hub — post once, share everywhere
  • Duplicate finder and smart cleanup tools
