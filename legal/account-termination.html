We may temporarily suspend your account or restrict features (including social publishing, cloud sync, or API access) when we reasonably believe:

  • You violated our Terms, Community Guidelines, Acceptable Use Policy, or applicable law
  • Your account shows signs of compromise, fraud, or unauthorized access
  • We received a valid legal request or credible rights-holder complaint
  • Your activity poses security, operational, or legal risk to the Service or other users
  • Payment disputes, chargebacks, or subscription abuse require investigation

During suspension, you may lose access to some or all features. We will attempt to notify you at your registered email when practicable, unless notification would compromise an investigation or is prohibited by law.

4. Permanent termination (ban)

We may permanently terminate accounts for serious or repeated violations, including but not limited to:

  • Publishing illegal content or content that exploits minors
  • Hate speech, credible threats, harassment campaigns, or doxing
  • Fraud, phishing, malware distribution, or systematic spam
  • Repeated copyright infringement after warnings
  • Circumventing suspensions through new accounts or devices
  • Reverse engineering, scraping, or attacking our infrastructure

Permanent bans are generally not reversible. We may block associated email addresses, payment methods, or device identifiers to prevent re-registration.

We may discontinue the Service or terminate accounts for convenience with reasonable notice where practicable. If we shut down the Service entirely, we will provide instructions for exporting data where technically feasible and will handle subscriptions according to our Refund & Subscription Policy and applicable store rules.

6. Subscriptions and billing after termination

Account termination does not automatically cancel subscriptions billed through Google Play, Apple, or Stripe. You must cancel subscriptions in the respective store or billing portal to stop recurring charges. Refunds after termination are governed by our Refund & Subscription Policy and each platform’s policies. We do not provide pro-rata refunds for partial billing periods except where required by law.
