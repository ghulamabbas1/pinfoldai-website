Security Disclaimer

Last updated: July 6, 2026 · Version 2026-07-06

Pinfold (“we”, “us”, “our”) takes reasonable measures to protect the Pinfold mobile application (com.pinfoldai.fileexplorer) and related API services at api.pinfoldai.com (collectively, the “Service”). This Security Disclaimer describes our security practices at a high level, their limitations, and your responsibilities. It supplements our Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, and License & Disclaimer.

1. No absolute security

No method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure. While we implement safeguards designed to protect the Service, we cannot guarantee that unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, loss, or destruction of data will never occur. You use the Service at your own risk.

2. Measures we employ

Depending on the feature and environment, we may use measures such as:

  • TLS/HTTPS encryption for data in transit between the App and our servers.
  • Encryption at rest for sensitive server-side data, including OAuth tokens and credentials.
  • Access controls, authentication, and role-based permissions for production systems.
  • Network segmentation, firewalls, and monitoring on cloud infrastructure (including Azure and Hetzner).
  • Regular patching and dependency updates for backend services built on .NET.
  • Rate limiting and abuse detection on public API endpoints.
  • Secure development practices for the Flutter mobile application.

We do not publicly disclose detailed security architecture, as doing so could assist attackers. We may describe practices at a summary level in security questionnaires for enterprise customers where appropriate.

3. Your responsibilities

Security is shared. You are responsible for:

  • Maintaining a strong, unique password and enabling multi-factor authentication where offered.
  • Protecting your device passcode, biometric unlock, and app lock PIN stored locally on your device.
  • Safeguarding vault passphrases and encryption keys — we cannot recover lost passphrases.
  • Keeping your operating system and the Pinfold App updated to the latest available version.
  • Not jailbreaking, rooting, or disabling device security features that protect the App.
  • Reviewing connected social and cloud account permissions and revoking access you no longer need.
  • Not sharing account credentials or OAuth authorizations with unauthorized persons.
  • Verifying the authenticity of emails and links claiming to be from Pinfold before entering credentials.

4. Local device security

Much of your file data remains on your device. Device loss, theft, malware, or unauthorized physical access may compromise local data regardless of our server-side protections. Use device encryption, remote wipe features, and app lock where available.

5. Third-party services

When you connect Google, Apple, Meta, Microsoft, TikTok, LinkedIn, X, YouTube, Threads, Dropbox, Stripe, or other providers, your data is also subject to their security practices. We are not responsible for security incidents originating at third-party services. See our Third-Party Services Disclaimer.

6. AI and automated features

AI features may process text and metadata you submit. Do not submit passwords, payment card numbers, government ID numbers, or other highly sensitive data to AI tools. AI output may contain unsafe instructions or inaccurate security guidance — always verify before acting. See our AI Disclaimer.

7. Incident response

If we become aware of a security incident affecting personal data in our control, we will investigate promptly and take reasonable steps to mitigate harm. Where required by applicable law, we will notify affected users and regulators within prescribed timeframes.

8. Reporting vulnerabilities

If you believe you have discovered a security vulnerability in the Service, please report it responsibly to support@pinfoldai.com. Do not publicly disclose vulnerabilities until we have had a reasonable opportunity to investigate and remediate. We appreciate good-faith reports and may acknowledge researchers at our discretion.

9. No fiduciary duty

Our security measures do not create a fiduciary or special relationship with you. We do not guarantee protection against all threats, including zero-day exploits, nation-state actors, or social engineering attacks targeting you personally.

10. Limitation of liability

To the maximum extent permitted by law, Pinfold is not liable for damages arising from unauthorized access, data breach, malware, phishing, or other security incidents except as expressly required by applicable law. Our aggregate liability is subject to the limitations in our Terms of Service and License & Disclaimer.

11. Changes

We may update security practices and this disclaimer as technology and threats evolve. Material changes will be reflected in the “Last updated” date above.

12. Contact

Security inquiries: support@pinfoldai.com
