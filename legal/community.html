Community Guidelines

Last updated: July 6, 2026 · Version 2026-07-06

These Community Guidelines (“Guidelines”) apply to everyone who uses Pinfold (the “App”), our website at pinfoldai.com, and related services (collectively, the “Service”). They explain the standards we expect when you interact with other users, publish content, or participate in any community-facing feature. These Guidelines supplement our Terms of Service and Acceptable Use Policy. If you violate these Guidelines, we may remove content, restrict features, suspend your account, or terminate your access.

1. Our community purpose

Pinfold is a productivity and business file-management platform. Our community standards are designed to keep the Service safe, professional, and useful for legitimate work. We do not tolerate behavior that harms others, abuses our systems, or undermines trust in the platform.

2. Be respectful

Treat other users, our support team, and third-party platform communities with respect. You must not:

  • Harass, bully, threaten, or intimidate any person or group
  • Use slurs, hate speech, or dehumanizing language based on race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, or age
  • Encourage violence or celebrate harm against individuals or groups
  • Dox, stalk, or share private information about others without consent
  • Impersonate Pinfold staff, other users, brands, or public figures

3. Keep content professional and lawful

Content you create, upload, schedule, or publish through the Service must comply with applicable law and remain suitable for a business productivity tool. Prohibited content includes, without limitation:

  • Pornography, sexually explicit material, or nudity intended primarily to arouse
  • Gambling, betting, casino promotions, or lottery scams
  • Illegal drugs, weapons trafficking, or instructions for criminal activity
  • Fraud, phishing, pyramid schemes, counterfeit goods, or deceptive advertising
  • Malware, spyware, or tools designed to compromise devices or accounts
  • Content that infringes copyright, trademark, privacy, or other intellectual property rights

4. Social publishing standards

When you connect social accounts and publish through Pinfold, you remain responsible for compliance with each platform’s terms, community rules, and applicable law. You must not use the Service to:

  • Spam, flood, or send unsolicited bulk messages
  • Manipulate engagement through fake accounts, bots, or paid click farms
  • Publish misleading health, financial, or political claims without appropriate disclosure
  • Circumvent rate limits, API restrictions, or platform enforcement actions
  • Re-publish content you do not have the right to share

See our Social Integration Policy for additional requirements.

5. AI-assisted features

If you use AI-generated captions, summaries, or suggestions, you are responsible for reviewing output before publishing. Do not use AI features to generate harassment, disinformation, impersonation, or other prohibited content. See our AI Disclaimer.

6. Protect minors

The Service is not directed at children under 13 (or 16 in the EEA). Do not create accounts for minors, solicit personal information from minors, or post content that exploits or endangers children. We report suspected child exploitation to appropriate authorities.

7. Security and account integrity

You must not:

  • Share account credentials or sell, rent, or transfer accounts without authorization
  • Attempt to access another user’s account, vault, or files without permission
  • Probe, scan, or test the vulnerability of our systems without written consent
  • Interfere with or disrupt the Service, including through denial-of-service attacks
  • Use automated scripts to scrape or harvest data except through documented APIs you are authorized to use

8. Commercial and promotional activity

Legitimate business promotion is permitted when it complies with these Guidelines and platform rules. You must clearly disclose sponsored or paid partnerships where required by law or platform policy. Unsolicited advertising in support channels, deceptive endorsements, and multi-level marketing schemes are prohibited.

9. Reporting violations

If you see content or behavior that violates these Guidelines, report it through the in-app Report feature or our contact form. Include relevant details: account name, links, screenshots, and a description of the issue. We review good-faith reports and may take action at our discretion.

10. Enforcement

We enforce these Guidelines in our reasonable discretion. Depending on severity and history, actions may include:

  • Warning or request to remove or edit content
  • Temporary feature restrictions (for example, pausing social publishing)
  • Content removal or account suspension
  • Permanent account termination
  • Referral to law enforcement or rights holders where appropriate

See Account Termination Policy for more detail on suspensions and bans.

11. Appeals

If you believe we made an error, you may appeal by emailing support@pinfoldai.com within 30 days of the action. Include your account email, a description of the action taken, and why you believe it was incorrect. We will review and respond within a reasonable time. Our decision after appeal is final except where applicable law requires otherwise.

12. Changes to these Guidelines

We may update these Guidelines to reflect new features, legal requirements, or community needs. Material changes will be reflected in the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated Guidelines.

13. Contact

Questions about these Guidelines: support@pinfoldai.com · Contact & complaints
