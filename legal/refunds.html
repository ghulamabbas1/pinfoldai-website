Refund & Subscription Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026 · Version 2026-07-06

This Refund & Subscription Policy (“Policy”) explains how paid plans, renewals, cancellations, and refunds work for Pinfold (the “App”) and related premium features (the “Service”). It supplements our Terms of Service. Subscriptions may be purchased through Google Play, Apple App Store, or Stripe (web or direct billing), depending on your platform and region.

1. Subscription plans

Pinfold may offer free and paid tiers. Paid subscriptions (“Premium” or similarly named plans) unlock productivity features such as expanded storage limits, social publishing quotas, cloud sync options, and other capabilities described in the App or on our website at purchase time. Plan names, prices, features, and billing intervals may vary by region and store.

2. Billing platforms

Your payment method and refund rights depend on where you subscribed:

  • Google Play (Android): billing and refunds are processed by Google under Google Play Terms and Google refund policies
  • Apple App Store (iOS): billing and refunds are processed by Apple under Apple Media Services Terms and Apple refund policies
  • Stripe (web or direct): billing is processed by Stripe on our behalf; refunds for eligible Stripe purchases are handled as described in Section 6

We do not store full payment card numbers. Payment processors collect and process payment data according to their privacy policies.

3. Auto-renewal

Unless stated otherwise at purchase, subscriptions automatically renew at the end of each billing period (monthly, annual, or other interval shown at checkout) until cancelled. You authorize the applicable store or Stripe to charge your payment method for each renewal at the then-current price, which may change with notice as permitted by store rules and our Terms.

4. Free trials and promotional offers

We may offer free trials or discounted introductory periods. If you do not cancel before the trial ends, you may be charged the standard subscription price shown at signup. Trial eligibility, duration, and conversion terms are displayed at enrollment. Store-specific trial rules (Google Play, Apple) apply in addition to this Policy.

5. How to cancel

You must cancel through the platform where you subscribed:

  • Google Play: Play Store → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Pinfold → Cancel
  • Apple: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Pinfold → Cancel (or use Apple’s subscription management)
  • Stripe: use the billing portal link in your receipt email, or email support@pinfoldai.com from your registered account email

Deleting the App or your Pinfold account does not automatically cancel subscription billing. Cancel separately to avoid future charges.

6. Refund requests

6.1 Google Play refunds

Request refunds through Google Play: Google Play refund help. Google decides refund eligibility under its policies. We cannot override Google’s refund decisions for Play-billed subscriptions.

6.2 Apple App Store refunds

Request refunds through Apple: Report a Problem or Apple Support. Apple decides refund eligibility. We cannot process refunds for Apple-billed purchases directly.

6.3 Stripe refunds

For subscriptions billed through Stripe, contact support@pinfoldai.com within 14 days of charge with your account email and transaction details. We may approve refunds at our discretion for duplicate charges, billing errors, or failure to deliver paid features due to a verified Service outage. We generally do not refund partial billing periods after you have used Premium features during that period, except where required by law.

7. Chargebacks and payment disputes

If you dispute a charge with your bank or payment provider without contacting us first, we may suspend your account pending investigation. Fraudulent chargebacks may result in permanent termination. We encourage you to email support before initiating a chargeback so we can resolve billing issues promptly.

8. Price changes

We may change subscription prices. For store-billed subscriptions, price changes are communicated through Google Play or Apple according to their rules; you may accept the new price or cancel before renewal. For Stripe subscriptions, we will provide advance notice by email where practicable. Continued subscription after a price change constitutes acceptance unless you cancel.

9. Feature changes and service availability

We may add, modify, or remove Premium features over time. Material reductions to core Premium functionality will be communicated when practicable. Refunds based solely on feature changes are not guaranteed except where required by applicable consumer law or store policy.

10. Account termination and subscriptions

If we terminate your account for violation of our Terms, you are not entitled to a refund for unused subscription time unless required by law. If you voluntarily delete your account, cancel your subscription separately. See Account Termination Policy.

11. Taxes

Prices may exclude or include taxes depending on region. Applicable sales tax, VAT, GST, or similar taxes may be added at checkout as determined by the billing platform and your location.

12. Regional consumer rights

Nothing in this Policy limits mandatory consumer rights under applicable law, including cooling-off periods or statutory refund rights in the European Union, United Kingdom, Australia, or other jurisdictions where they cannot be waived by contract. Where store policies provide greater protection, those policies apply to store-billed purchases.

13. Contact

Billing and refund questions: support@pinfoldai.com · Contact & complaints
