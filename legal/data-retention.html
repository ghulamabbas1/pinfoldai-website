Data Retention Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026 · Version 2026-07-06

This Data Retention Policy describes how long Pinfold (“we”, “us”, “our”) retains information processed through the Pinfold mobile application (com.pinfoldai.fileexplorer) and related services at api.pinfoldai.com (collectively, the “Service”). It supplements our Privacy Policy and Data Deletion page.

1. General principles

  • We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the Service, comply with law, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.
  • Retention periods may vary by data category, feature used, subscription status, and legal jurisdiction.
  • When retention ends, we delete or anonymize data using commercially reasonable methods, subject to technical limitations and legal holds.
  • Backup systems may retain deleted data for a limited period before automatic purge.

2. Retention schedule

The table below summarizes typical retention periods. These are guidelines, not guarantees; specific records may be kept longer when required by law, litigation hold, fraud investigation, or platform dispute resolution.

Data category Typical retention period Notes
Account profile (email, display name, country, preferences) While account is active + up to 30 days after deletion request Deleted or anonymized after account deletion completes, unless legal hold applies.
Authentication credentials & session tokens Session: hours to 30 days; refresh tokens: until logout, revocation, or account deletion Revoked immediately on password change or explicit sign-out of all devices where supported.
OAuth tokens (social & cloud providers) While connection is active; deleted within 30 days of disconnect or account deletion Includes Meta, Google, Microsoft, TikTok, LinkedIn, X, YouTube, Threads, Dropbox, and similar integrations.
Social publishing queue & schedule metadata Until published or cancelled + up to 90 days Failed jobs and logs may be retained briefly for troubleshooting.
Uploaded media for social publishing (server copies) Until publish completes + up to 30 days Temporary staging only; not a long-term media archive.
AI feature inputs & outputs (server-processed) Up to 30 days, or not retained where processing is ephemeral Some AI requests are processed in memory without persistent storage. See Privacy Policy.
Subscription & billing records Life of account + 7 years (or longer if required by tax law) Processed via Google Play, Apple App Store, or Stripe; subject to their retention as well.
Support tickets & feedback Up to 3 years after case closure May be retained longer if related to an open dispute or legal matter.
Abuse reports & moderation records Up to 5 years Retained to enforce policies and respond to repeat violations.
Security & access logs (server/API) 90 days to 12 months May include IP address, timestamp, endpoint, and error codes; generally not linked to file contents.
Anonymized analytics & aggregated metrics Up to 26 months Does not identify individual users after anonymization.
Local device data (vault, cache, app lock PIN) Until you delete the App, clear data, or wipe the device Stored on your device; not uploaded unless you enable sync or cloud features.
Legal hold & regulatory records As required by applicable law or court order Overrides standard deletion schedules while the hold is in effect.

3. Account deletion

When you delete your account, we begin removing server-side personal data within our standard processing window, typically within 30 days. Some data may persist in encrypted backups for up to 90 days before automatic purge. For step-by-step instructions, see Data Deletion.

4. Disconnecting third-party services

Disconnecting a social or cloud provider removes our authorization to access that service and triggers deletion of associated OAuth tokens within the periods above. Content already stored on the third-party platform remains under that platform’s policies.

5. Inactive accounts

We may identify accounts with no login or meaningful activity for an extended period (typically 24 months or more). We may send a notice to your registered email before restricting features or deleting inactive account data, where practicable and permitted by law.

6. Your choices

  • Delete your account in Settings to initiate server-side deletion.
  • Disconnect unused social and cloud integrations to reduce stored tokens.
  • Exercise privacy rights available in your region as described in our Privacy Policy.
  • Contact us if you need assistance with a deletion or export request.

7. International users

Retention practices are designed to align with GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, LGPD, PIPEDA, and other applicable frameworks. Where local law requires a shorter retention period, we apply the shorter period to the extent required.

8. Changes

We may update retention periods as our Service, infrastructure, or legal obligations evolve. Material changes will be reflected in the “Last updated” date above and, where required, communicated through the App or email.

Data retention inquiries: support@pinfoldai.com
