Children’s Privacy (COPPA)

Last updated: July 6, 2026 · Version 2026-07-06

This Children’s Privacy policy (“Policy”) explains how Pinfold (“we”, “us”, “our”) addresses the privacy of children in connection with the Pinfold mobile application (com.pinfoldai.fileexplorer), our website, and related services (collectively, the “Service”). It supplements our Privacy Policy and complies with the U.S. Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (“COPPA”) where applicable.

1. Service not directed to children

Pinfold is a productivity and business file-management tool intended for general audiences, not for children. We do not knowingly collect, solicit, or retain personal information from children under 13 years of age (or under 16 in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or other regions where a higher age applies).

2. Age requirement

You must be at least 13 years old (or the minimum age required in your country to consent to use of information services) to create an account or use the Service. If you are between 13 and the age of majority in your jurisdiction, you may use the Service only with the consent and supervision of a parent or legal guardian who agrees to our Terms on your behalf.

3. No knowing collection from children under 13

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Personal information includes name, email, phone number, persistent identifiers, photos, geolocation, and similar data that could identify a child. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without verified parental consent, we will delete that information promptly and may terminate the associated account.

4. Parental rights under COPPA

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided personal information to us, you may contact us to:

  • Review what personal information we have collected from your child, if any
  • Request deletion of your child’s personal information
  • Refuse further collection or use of your child’s information

Email support@pinfoldai.com with subject “COPPA parental request” and include your relationship to the child, the child’s account email if known, and the nature of your request. We may ask for information to verify your identity and relationship before fulfilling the request.

5. School and institutional use

The Service is not designed for use by schools or institutions as an educational platform for children under 13. If an institution believes a student under 13 has accessed the Service, the institution or parent should contact us so we can delete any associated personal information.

6. Features and content standards

We prohibit content that exploits or endangers minors. Accounts used to share, solicit, or store child sexual abuse material (CSAM) are permanently banned and reported to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) or appropriate authorities as required by law. See our Community Guidelines and Acceptable Use Policy.

7. Advertising to children

We do not knowingly display behaviorally targeted advertising to children. The Service does not include third-party gambling or adult advertising. Any future advertising features will comply with applicable child privacy laws and store policies.

8. App store age ratings

Pinfold is listed on Google Play and the Apple App Store with age ratings reflecting its productivity focus. Parents should use device-level parental controls and store family features to manage downloads and purchases by minors.

9. International minors

In the EEA, UK, and other regions, GDPR and local laws may require parental consent for processing personal data of children below age 16 (or lower age set by member state law, not below 13). We apply the higher applicable age threshold and do not knowingly process children’s data without appropriate consent.

10. Changes

We may update this Policy to reflect legal or operational changes. Material updates will be reflected in the “Last updated” date above.

11. Contact

Children’s privacy inquiries: support@pinfoldai.com · Contact & complaints
