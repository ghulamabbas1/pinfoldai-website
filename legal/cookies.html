Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Pinfold AI (“Pinfold,” “we,” “us”) uses cookies and similar technologies on our website at pinfoldai.com and in connection with the Pinfold mobile application (com.pinfoldai.fileexplorer). It should be read together with our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

1. What are cookies and similar technologies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your browser or device when you visit a website. Similar technologies include local storage, session storage, pixels, SDK identifiers, and device IDs used in mobile apps. They help websites and apps remember preferences, keep sessions secure, and understand how services are used.

2. Who sets cookies?

Cookies may be set by Pinfold (first-party cookies) or by service providers acting on our behalf (third-party cookies). Third-party providers are bound by contractual obligations consistent with this policy and applicable law.

3. How we use cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Enable core website functionality and security
  • Remember language and display preferences
  • Measure website traffic and performance with privacy-conscious analytics
  • Understand App crashes and usage patterns where analytics are enabled
  • Manage cookie consent in regions where consent is required

We do not use cookies for third-party advertising networks on our marketing site. We do not sell cookie data.

4. Types of cookies we use

We classify cookies by purpose:

  • Strictly necessary: required for site operation, security, and consent storage; cannot be disabled without breaking core functionality
  • Functional: remember choices such as language selection
  • Analytics: help us understand visits and App performance in aggregated form
  • Marketing: not used on our site at this time; if introduced, we will update this policy and request consent where required

5. Marketing website cookies

Our marketing website uses a minimal set of cookies. We prioritize privacy and avoid invasive tracking. Typical cookies on pinfoldai.com include:

We do not deploy advertising, retargeting, or social media tracking pixels on the marketing site unless this policy is updated and consent is obtained where required.

6. Mobile app analytics and identifiers

The Pinfold App does not use browser cookies. It may use mobile SDKs and device identifiers for crash reporting, performance monitoring, and aggregated usage analytics where enabled by your device settings and applicable platform policies. Data is minimized and not used for cross-app advertising by Pinfold.

  • Crash logs may include device model, OS version, app version, and stack traces
  • Usage events may include feature interactions in anonymized or pseudonymized form
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely and are not shared with analytics providers
  • You may limit ad tracking through iOS and Android system settings; see platform documentation

7. Third-party cookies and embedded content

If we embed third-party content (such as app store badges or OAuth login on web properties), those providers may set their own cookies subject to their policies. We do not control third-party cookies. Review Google, Apple, Facebook, and Stripe privacy policies when using their login or payment flows.

8. Legal bases (EEA / UK)

In the EEA and UK, we use strictly necessary cookies based on legitimate interests and contractual necessity. Non-essential cookies (such as analytics) are used only with your consent where required. You may withdraw consent at any time through our cookie banner or browser settings, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

9. Managing cookies on the website

You can manage cookies through:

  • Our cookie consent banner (where displayed) to accept or reject non-essential cookies
  • Browser settings to block, delete, or notify you about cookies
  • Clearing local storage through browser developer tools or privacy settings

Blocking strictly necessary cookies may prevent parts of the website from functioning correctly.

10. Browser-specific controls

Most browsers allow cookie management through settings. Consult your browser help documentation for Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, or other browsers. Mobile browsers offer similar controls in privacy settings.

11. Do Not Track and Global Privacy Control

We do not respond to Do Not Track signals because no industry standard governs how to interpret them. Where applicable law requires, we honor Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals as an opt-out of sale/sharing. Pinfold does not sell personal data collected through cookies.

12. California notice

California residents may have rights regarding personal information collected through cookies under the CCPA/CPRA. Because we do not sell or share cookie data for cross-context behavioral advertising, opt-out of sale/sharing is generally not applicable. Contact support@pinfoldai.com for California privacy requests.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy when we change technologies or legal requirements. Updates will be reflected in the version date above. Material changes may be communicated through our website or App where appropriate.
