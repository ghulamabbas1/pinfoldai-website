Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026 · Version 2026-07-06

This Acceptable Use Policy (“AUP”) describes permitted and prohibited uses of the Pinfold mobile application (com.pinfoldai.fileexplorer), API at api.pinfoldai.com, and related services (the “Service”). It is incorporated into our Terms of Service. Violations may result in content removal, feature restrictions, Account suspension, or permanent ban.

1. Purpose and scope

Pinfold is a productivity and business file management tool for Android and iOS. The Service helps users organize files, connect cloud storage (Google Drive, OneDrive, Dropbox), publish to social platforms through official APIs, and use AI-assisted features. This AUP applies to all users, content, and activity on the Service.

2. Permitted uses

You may use the Service to:

  • Browse, organize, and manage files on your Device for personal or business productivity
  • Sync files with cloud providers you authorize
  • Publish lawful, professional content to social platforms you connect
  • Use AI features to assist with captions, organization, and workflows in compliance with this AUP
  • Collaborate within the limits of your Subscription and applicable law

3. Professional standards

Users must maintain professional and lawful conduct. Captions, filenames, folder names, and published content should be appropriate for a business productivity context. Vulgar, obscene, or gratuitously offensive language and imagery are prohibited, even if not explicitly listed elsewhere in this AUP.

4. Prohibited content — sexual and adult material

You may not use the Service to create, store, sync, publish, or distribute:

  • Pornography or sexually explicit material intended to arouse
  • Nudity in a sexual context, including exploitative or non-consensual imagery
  • Escort services, adult entertainment promotions, or sexual services advertising
  • Content sexualizing minors in any form (zero tolerance; reported to authorities)

5. Prohibited content — gambling and betting

You may not use the Service to promote, facilitate, or distribute:

  • Online gambling, betting, casino, or lottery promotions where unlawful or restricted
  • Tips, schemes, or affiliate funnels primarily aimed at gambling revenue
  • Misleading “guaranteed win” or get-rich-quick gambling content

6. Prohibited content — illegal activity

You may not use the Service for any unlawful purpose, including:

  • Violations of criminal law, sanctions, export controls, or intellectual property rights
  • Distribution of stolen data, pirated software, or unauthorized access tools
  • Drug trafficking, weapons trafficking, or other regulated goods where prohibited
  • Facilitation of fraud, money laundering, or tax evasion

7. Prohibited content — harmful and abusive conduct

The following are prohibited:

  • Hate speech, harassment, threats, bullying, or incitement to violence
  • Content glorifying terrorism, self-harm, or violence against individuals or groups
  • Doxxing, stalking, or publishing private personal information without consent
  • Discrimination based on protected characteristics where prohibited by law

8. Prohibited content — deceptive and malicious activity

You may not:

  • Send spam, bulk unsolicited messages, or automated posting that violates platform rules
  • Phish for credentials, distribute malware, ransomware, or spyware
  • Run pyramid schemes, multi-level marketing scams, or deceptive investment offers
  • Impersonate Pinfold, other users, brands, or public figures
  • Publish false or misleading advertisements, fake reviews, or manipulated media intended to deceive

9. Social publishing rules

When using social publishing features, you must:

  • Comply with each connected platform’s terms, community guidelines, and advertising policies
  • Disclose sponsored or paid content where required by law and platform rules
  • Respect copyright, trademark, and publicity rights in media you publish
  • Avoid automated posting patterns that constitute spam or platform manipulation
  • Use official APIs only; do not attempt to bypass platform rate limits or restrictions

10. AI feature use

AI features must not be used to:

  • Generate unlawful, harmful, or prohibited content described in this AUP
  • Impersonate real individuals or create deceptive deepfakes
  • Circumvent content moderation, platform policies, or legal restrictions
  • Process highly sensitive personal data without lawful basis and user consent

You are responsible for reviewing AI-generated outputs before publishing or acting on them.

11. File management and cloud sync

When managing or syncing files, you must not:

  • Store or distribute malware, exploit code, or tools primarily designed for unauthorized access
  • Use cloud sync to host public file-sharing or warez repositories violating third-party terms
  • Exceed reasonable storage or bandwidth limits through abusive automated syncing
  • Upload content you do not have the right to store or share

12. Account integrity and security

You must not:

  • Create multiple Accounts to evade enforcement, limits, or bans
  • Share credentials except with authorized team members under valid business use
  • Attempt unauthorized access to the Service, other Accounts, or connected Third-Party Services
  • Probe, scan, or test vulnerabilities without prior written authorization from Pinfold
  • Interfere with Service operation through excessive requests, bots, or denial-of-service techniques

13. Intellectual property

Respect intellectual property rights. Do not upload, sync, or publish content that infringes copyright, trademark, trade secret, or other proprietary rights. Respond promptly to valid takedown notices and platform rights reports. Repeat infringers may have Accounts terminated.

14. Third-party platform compliance

The Service integrates with Google, Apple, Facebook, Microsoft, Dropbox, Stripe, and social platforms through official APIs. Your use must comply with all applicable third-party developer policies. Pinfold is not responsible for enforcement actions taken by those platforms against your accounts.

15. Advertising and monetization

Pinfold does not display third-party gambling or adult advertising in the App. Premium subscriptions unlock productivity features only. You may not use the Service to monetize prohibited content categories described in this AUP. Any future optional advertising will comply with app store policies and this AUP.

16. Automated filtering and monitoring

We may use automated filters, keyword detection, and human review to identify prohibited content before or after publishing. Filters may block captions or uploads that appear to violate this AUP. Automated decisions may not be perfect; contact support if you believe content was blocked in error.

17. Reporting violations

Report abusive users, prohibited content, or security concerns through the in-app Report feature or our contact form. Include relevant details (username, timestamps, screenshots) to help us investigate. We may share reports with law enforcement or platform partners where appropriate.

18. Enforcement

Violations may result in:

  • Removal or blocking of specific content
  • Temporary restriction of publishing, sync, or AI features
  • Account suspension pending investigation
  • Permanent Account termination without refund where permitted by law and store policy
  • Reporting to law enforcement, app stores, or connected platforms

We may take action without prior notice where necessary to protect users, comply with law, or prevent ongoing harm.

19. Appeals

If your content was removed or your Account was restricted, you may appeal by emailing support@pinfoldai.com with your Account email, description of the action taken, and explanation of why you believe the decision was incorrect. We will review appeals in a reasonable timeframe but are not obligated to reinstate content or Accounts that violate this AUP.

20. Changes to this policy

We may update this AUP to address new risks, features, or legal requirements. Updates will be reflected in the version date above. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance.
